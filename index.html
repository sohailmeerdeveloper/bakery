Our story

From our oven, with care.

Heavenly Layers began with a single home oven and one promise — every bake leaves the kitchen only if we'd serve it at our own table.

Today we hand-craft six signature ranges using locally sourced butter, single-origin chocolate and zero artificial flavourings. Every box is packed by the same hands that baked it.
