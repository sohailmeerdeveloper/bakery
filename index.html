Notes from the counter.

Heavenly Layers is a small bakery in the corner of a busier street. Six things come out of the oven on any given week, and every one of them is logged: order number, month, product, quantity, price.

This page is the running ledger of that habit. It reads from a single array of rows in JavaScript memory and derives every metric, every chart and every formula from those rows — so the day the prices change, the day a brownie sells twice as fast, the page updates itself.

Key findings.

  1. 01Donut box has the highest total sales.
  2. 02Sales increased from January to July.
  3. 03Cookies had lower sales compared to cakes and donuts.
  4. 04Bakery products were sold consistently every month.
